Welcome to Dignan's Cafe & Catering

History

Opened in 2012, Dignan's Cafe & Catering has established itself as one of the best catering & cafe businesses in Edinburgh. Over the past twelve years we have been serving local businesses on the catering side and the public through our cafe. At Dignan's our mission is to provide exceptional customer service and a comfortable open environment where people can enjoy the simple pleasures of life whether that's savouring a cup of their favourite brewed coffee, catching up with friends/family or working in a peaceful space. Our aim is to build a strong sense of community by supplying bespoke catering to local businesses, artists and events. We also aim for strong brand recognition in both our presentation and service.

Catering

Catering is our main business. We service local businesses, artists and events in Edinburgh. We do office lunches, corporate catering, corporate barbeques. Our office catering and office barbeques can be found on our catering page. This is our most popular and in demand servicing the financial hub of Edinburgh. We also do personalized catering for private party catering and wedding catering and sometimes do personalized office catering.
