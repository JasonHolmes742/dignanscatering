Events
  • Corporate Catering: Impress your collegues and clients with our professionaly catered lunches, meetings and events such as executive lunches, product launches, conferences, team-building events and VIP client dinners. This is our most popular and most in demand service. Our Catering website page is dedicated to our office catering packages.
  • Private Party Catering: We use our specialized menus service to suit whatever the event type or budget is for birthdays, anniveraries, weddings and other special occasions. Whether it's a birthday, a baby shower, or a holiday gathering, we can create a menu that fits your personal style.
  • Wedding Catering: Celebrate your special day with mouthwatering food and exceptional-service. We offer bespoke catering options, from sit down dinners to buffet-style spreads, all customized to fit your event's theme and special dietary needs.
Food & Beverage
  • Full-Service Catering: This all inclusive service handles everything from food preparation and delivery to setup, service, and cleanup. Ideal for large events like weddings, corporate galas, and upscale parties. We provide full event staffing for this kind of service.
  • Gourmet Menu Planning: We can work with you and your guests to create customs menus tailored to your tastes, dietary needs, and the theme of your event. This can include multi-course meals, specialized diets or signature dishes curated specifically for the event.
  • Premium Beverage Service: Premium drink options, including wine pairings, signature cocktails, craft beer selections, and non-alcoholic beverages.
  • Food & Beverage Tastings: We offer tasting sessions for our clients to try different menu options and finalize their selections before the event. These tastings may include wine pairings and are usually done in a private setting with the chef and staff.
Event Staffing
Our professionally trained staff, including waiters, bartenders, chefs, kitchen porters and event coordinators are there to ensure your special event runs smoothly and that the guests receive top-notch service throughout.
Cafe
We have a medium sized cafe located within the corporate districts of Edinburgh serving the local and bustling tourist population. Dignan's Cafe offers a wide selection of freshly brewed coffee, teas, pastries, cakes, soups, salads and more. Our pastries and cakes are baked daily right in our cafe. From buttery croissants to indulgent cheesecakes, our sweet and savoury treats are perfect for pairing with your drink. Enjoy a rotating menu of healthy sandwiches, sweet and creamy soups, vibrant salads all made with fresh locally sourced ingredients. Our cafe menus can be found on our websites menus page.
Cleaning
At Dignan's Cafe & Catering, we understand that a successful event is not only about great food and service but also about creating a clean and welcoming environment for your guests. That's why we offer a comprehensive cleaning service as part of our catering packages. Our professional cleaning team ensures that your event remains spotless before, during, and after the event. We take care of everything from setting up and cleaning serving stations to clearing away used dishes and utensils. After your event, our team will thoroughly clean the venue, including wiping down surfaces, removing trash, and ensuring the space is left in pristine condition. Whether it's a corporate event, wedding, or private party, we handle all aspects of cleanliness so you can focus on enjoying the occasion. With Dignan's Cafe & Catering you can trust that every detail is covered leaving you with nothing to worry about.
